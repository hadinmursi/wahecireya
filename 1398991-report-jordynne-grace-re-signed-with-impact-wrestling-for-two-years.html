Report: Jordynne Grace Re-Signed With IMPACT Wrestling For Two Years

August 17, 2024
post

Jordynne Grace is returning to IMPACT Wrestling, and more information has come to light.

Grace, a former IMPACT Knockouts World Champion, reportedly entered free agency after the IMPACT Under Siege event on May 26. During her time with the promotion, she also held the Knockous Tag Team Championship and the Digital Media Championship.

During IMPACT Emergence on August 27, a video package confirmed that Grace will be returning to the company at the Victory Road event on September 8.

Mike Johnson of PWInsider reports that, per IMPACT sources, Grace signed a new deal with the company months ago, but she took some time off. She then set Victory Road as the date for her return.

Per Sean Ross Sapp of Fightful Select, Grace has re-signed with IMPACT for two years. Sapp wrote that, per sources, Grace got a significant raise, and she will also get time off for bodybuilding competitions.
