Brock Lesnar Rips A Door Off A Truck, Says Roman Reigns Has Unleashed The Bipolar Beast

August 29, 2024
post

“The Beast” has been unleashed.

To open the March 18 episode of WWE SmackDown, WWE Champion swerved WWE Universal Champion Roman Reigns and his “Special Counsel” Paul Heyman by texting the latter that he would be unable to make the show due to travel issues before he subsequently revealed that he would be coming to the show nonetheless. Reigns and The Usos fled the scene, and they tried to drive away, but a forklift-driving Lesnar, smiling with vengeful joy, caught the trio by surprise.

Lesnar slammed the forklift into the car; in doing so, he seemingly caused glass to hit The Usos upon impact.

Determined to escape Lesnar’s wrath, The Bloodline sought safety by running to another vehicle. The WWE Champion chased them down and ripped a door off the truck, but Reigns and his cousins still managed to get away.

Lesnar then carried the door to the ring and vowed that he would finally get revenge when he meets Reigns in the ring at WWE WrestleMania 38. He called himself “the Bipolar Beast” and make it clear that Reigns would get what was coming to him.

The two powerhouses will collide in a Winner Take All Championship Unification Match at WrestleMania 38.
