Whoppa Wit Da Choppa, age still in the 20s, is a musical artist who recently exploded into popularity when he featured in the song ‘Who I smoke (A Thousand Miles Remix)’ by Spinabenz, with Yuneggn Ace and FastMoney Goon.

Whoopa Wit Da Choppa: Wikipedia

Whoopa Wit Da Choppa was born in Jacksonville and currently lives in Florida, United States.

Anthony is currently known for his musical art and his part in ‘Who I smoke‘, but before that, he used to work regular jobs and even went to prison at least 2 times.

But, before his nickname is what it currently is today, the first nickname he had was ‘Ducci‘ as he revealed in an interview with DJ Smallz where his name went through more changes, to BigWop than to where it is now.

Whoppa Wit Da Choppa: Family Story

Anthony’s family consists of his mother, a cosmetologist, his father, brother and according to his Instagram a nephew. All of whom are unnamed. 

Anthony lost his tooth in his teenage years when he was playing basketball and someone on a bike hit him, accidentally, while playing and sent him falling flat on his face. Anthony also got his first job due to his Aunt, who gave him a job as a Summer Cam Instructor when he was 15/16 years old.

Since then, he has worked in UPS, a T-shirt printing company, in Construction work as a laborer, in J.E.A plant as a Scaffolder.

He has also gone to jail a minimum of 2 times, with one of them being for 1 year.
