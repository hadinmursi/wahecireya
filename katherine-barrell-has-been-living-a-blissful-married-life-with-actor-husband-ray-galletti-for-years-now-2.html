Katherine Barrell Has Been Living A Blissful Married Life With Actor Husband Ray Galletti For Years

August 20, 2024
post

Katherine Barrell has been married to her husband Ray Galletti, since 2017.  Ray is also a fellow actor.

They celebrated their fifth anniversary not long ago. The couple makes the most out of their time with the family. They celebrate Halloween, Christmas, and other holidays together as a family.

Katherine is certainly very proud of Ray as a father and a husband. She also acknowledged him as the best dad to her son on the occasion of Father’s day.

Since they both work in the movies, the two have to coordinate their schedules to make time for some family time.

Katherine has had a lucrative acting career and has been enjoying a prosperous life with her beloved husband and their loving child.

Meet Katherine Barrell’s Husband Ray Galletti

Wynonna Earp star Katherine Barrell met her now husband Ray Galletti on the set of My Ex-Ex.

The couple engaged in 2016 and tied the knot in December 2017 and has a loving son together.

Ray Galletti Age and Biography

Galletti was born on July 21, 1974, in Montreal, Quebec, Canada, and is forty-eight years old.

He found his passion for acting at a young when he landed the lead role of Danny Zuko in his High School’s theatre production of “Grease.”

He attended and graduated from the prestigious John Abbott theatre program. Ray has played in many numerous television films and series since his debut with the 1996 movie Home Song, directed by Nancy Malone.

Ray met his future wife, Katherine, on the set of My Ex-Ex where played the role of Ted Rosen while the actress portrayed Mary. They eventually fell in love with each other and went public after dating for quite a while.

His Career As An Actor

Galletti made his debut playing the role of Frat Boy #3 in the movie Home Song in 1996. He earned his first film break when he was cast as part of the ensemble as a young thief framed for murder in Obstruction of Justice.

Dedicated to pursuing his burgeoning film and television career, Galletti moved to Vancouver, British Columbia. There he spent many years building an impressive resume both on stage and in front of the camera.

His film credits include Are We Thre Yet with Ice Cube, The Core alongside Oscar-winning Hilary Swank, and I Spy with Eddie Murphy and Owen Wilson. On the small screen, he gained fame for his guest-starring role in the critically acclaimed series Da Vinci’s Inquest.

Ray has guest-starred in many other popular shows like iZombie, Arrow, and Rookie Blue. He came to Los Angeles for the first time after landing a role in the Sundance hit “Relax… It’s JustSex” alongside Oscar-nominated actress Jennifer Tilly,

He then had recurring roles in the FOX series Desire and Saints and Sinners. Moreover, Ray landed the leading role of David in one of MTV’s first-ever web series, Connected. He played alongside Ashley Peldon and Fox MacLachlan.

His other television credits include Smallville, Rookie Blue, Exposed, Arrow, The Listener, Hemlock Grove, The Art of More, Mishka, Crawford, Ransom, Private Eyes, Second for Love, Pretty Hard Cases, and Workin’ Moms.

Katherine Barrell And Ray Galletti Have A Loving Son

Katherine Barrell and Ray Galletti welcomed their first child, son Ronin Barrell Galletti, on September 8, 2021.

On Ronin’s first birthday, Katherine shared a sweet message on her Instagram. With a joyous picture of the little bundle of joy, the actress expressed her love and adoration for her child. She further thanked the community for all the love and support.

The actress captioned the post, “On Ronin’s first birthday I want to extend a giant, heart-swelled THANK YOU to this incredible community for all the love, positivity, support, advice, and well wishes you have sent to our family this year.”

Katherine And Ray Spark Their Romance On Instagram

Katherine occasionally posts about her beloved husband on social media. She shares some of the most lovable birthday shoutouts to Ray via Instagram.

“This photo perfectly encapsulates Ray!” Barrell shared in July 2020, before uploading a story how Ray hilariously looked on the bright side of things while touring an apartment with a small kitchen where a refrigerator wouldn’t even fit in it.

She ended the post by saying, “Ray, this is the light you bring to my life.” “So please join me in sending lots of light to this ray of sunshine today! HAPPY BIRTHDAY !!!”

On December 30, 2020, the pair celebrated their anniversary with a happy facetime oyster shucking masterclass with Tim Ronzon. With a loving selfie, Katherine captioned the post, “Anniversary, but make it lockdown. and a super special thank you to Mr. @realtimrozon for the facetime oyster shucking masterclass!!!”

Most recently on the special occasion of Father’s Day, Katherine shared a loving picture of her husband and her father. She captioned the post, “Beyond blessed, lucky, and grateful to have these TWO incredible Fathers in my life. One to me and one to my sweet boy.”

She Also Has A Dog

Katherine is an avid dog lover and has a cute dog named Bernie. She frequently shares cute posts of herself with her dog on her Instagram.

On November 1, 2022, Katherine shared a joyous picture of her family -her husband, her son, and her dog, on Halloween. She captioned the post, “Grandma made the duck costume so we made the farm.” The actress was holding her baby while Ray was carrying Bernie.

While not working, Katherine loves spending time with her family and going on morning walk with Bernie. On October 1, she shared a picture of them clicked from their Saturday morning walk. She wrote, “Saturday morning with my first born.”
