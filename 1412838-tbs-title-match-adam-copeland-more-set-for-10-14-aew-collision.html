TBS Title Match, Adam Copeland, More Set For 10/14 AEW Collision

August 20, 2024
post

All Elite Wrestling is loading up the card for the October 14 episode of AEW Collision.

The show was already set to feature two title matches. Another one was added to the lineup on the October 13 edition of AEW Rampage. After Skye Blue defeated Emi Sakura, AEW announced that she will challenge TBS Champion Kris Statlander for the title on Saturday.

AEW also announced that Adam Copeland will appear on the show. Copeland defeated Luchasaurus in his AEW in-ring debut on the October 10 episode of AEW Dynamite.

Elsewhere on the show, Komander will take on Brian Cage, and AEW World Tag Team Champions Ricky Starks and Big Bill will speak.

10/14 AEW Collision Card

The updated card for Saturday’s episode of AEW Collision can be seen below.

  •  Kris Statlander (c) vs. Skye Blue
  • : Christian Cage (c) vs. Bryan Danielson
  • : Samoa Joe (c) vs. Willie Mack
  • Brian Cage vs. Komander
  • Adam Copeland will appear live
  • We’ll hear from AEW World Tag Team Champions Ricky Starks & Big Bill
