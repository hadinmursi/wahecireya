Rick Springfield Biography

Rick Springfield is an Australian musician and actor. He is famously recognized for being a member of the pop-rock group Zoot from 1969 to 1971. He then left the group to ride solo, and took off his career with the single, “Speak to the Sky”. He moved to the United States and released the single “Jessie’s Girl”, which gave him international recognition, being a No. 1 hit in both Australia and the United States. He has also appeared in widely known TV Shows like; Supernatural, American Horror Story, Californication, among others.
