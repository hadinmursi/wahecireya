Vicente was born on 17 February 1940 to his parents in the suburb of Huentitán El Alto in Guadalajara, Jalisco. He spent his early years on his father Ramon’s ranch on the outskirts. 

His mother often took him to see the films of Pedro Infante and he picked up the guitar singing in the style of the ranchera singers he heard on the radio. 

He worked as a waiter, dishwasher, cashier, and finally manager of his uncle’s restaurant at a young age.

Vicente Fernández estimated net worth is $25 million. Most of his earnings are attributed form his career as an artist.

He earned his income through many Latin films and performances on stage as a singer and recordings and tours.

Vicente initiated his career as a street singer, later becoming a Mexican icon, recording more than 50 albums and working in movies.

He received three Grammy awards from thirteen nominations by the National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences of the United States.
