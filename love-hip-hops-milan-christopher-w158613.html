Love &amp; Hip Hops Milan Christopher: Miles Brock Is a Liar, Not Bisexual

August 12, 2024
post

Not so fast! Milan Christopher wasn’t about to let his ex Miles Brock off the hook for his comments to Us Weekly from earlier this month. The Love & Hip Hop star filmed an exclusive interview with Us to tell his side of the story. Spoiler alert: It’s completely opposite from what Miles had to say.

“When Miles was here, me and Miles were actually not up in the air, we were completely done,” Milan said, claiming that he broke up with Miles at the VH1 show’s reunion. “Miles is a liar. He lies about a lot of things.”

Following the televised breakup, Milan found himself face to face with his ex at a party, where he claims Miles brought a team of friends.

“He swung, and he hit me,” Milan said.

After the fight, the reality star said he put on some makeup to cover his black eye for an event, and when it was over, he went right to the police and had Miles arrested on charges of assault. Their brawl came after months of what Milan claimed were constant lies from Miles.

“I ended up finding out Miles had been out for years before I moved to Los Angeles,” he said of his ex, who claimed he hadn’t come out yet while on the show. “The fact that he was cheating on me, and when he physically put his hands on me, that was the nail in the coffin.”

As for Miles’ 10-year, on-off romance with Amber Hunt, Milan thinks it’s all for show.

“Miles is gay,” he told Us firmly. “He’s not bisexual.”
