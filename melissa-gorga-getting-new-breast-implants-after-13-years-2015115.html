Melissa Gorga Getting New Breast Implants After 13 Years

August 19, 2024
post

Keeping it “real”! Melissa Gorga got candid with her 744,000 Instagram followers on Monday, May 11, when she revealed that she was going under the knife — again — to have her breast implants replaced.

The Real Housewives of New Jersey star, 36, shared a photo of herself before the procedure early Monday morning. In it, she flashes two thumbs-up at the camera while wearing a patient gown and posing with her plastic surgeon.

“Wish me luck!” the married mother of three captioned the pic. “Following the Dr’s orders… After 13 years they say it’s always best to switch out your breast implants 🙂 so here we go… I know I’m in good hands with @drfiorillo #gummybears” 

Gorga has not talked much about plastic surgery, but based on her caption, it appears she had a breast augmentation when she was in her early 20s, a few years before she had her first child, Antonia, 9, with husband Joe Gorga. (The couple are also parents to son Gino, 7, and Joey, 5.)
