Selena Gomezs Best Red Carpet Moments and Style Evolution

September 09, 2024
post

Selena Gomez’s fashion evolution is undeniably fabulous.

Kicking off her career as a child on Barney & Friends, the actress soon became a Disney Channel star as the face of Wizards of Waverly Place. During the mid-2000s, Gomez showed off her youthful sense of style, including skinny jeans, long tank tops, converse and more.

After retiring from Disney, her style matured and began to include bespoke gowns with plunging necklines and daring silhouettes.

Previously styled by Kate Young, Gomez recently switched to celebrity stylist Erin Walsh, who elevated her wardrobe to include elegant and vibrant pieces that flatter her figure.
