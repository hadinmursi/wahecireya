Greg Smithey is a fitness and aerobics teacher from the United States. When Smithey is working out, he includes the lower body strengthening routines that he used to do when training for vaulting. VHS tapes of Smithey’s exercise were sold in excess of a million copies worldwide. On Smithey’s list of projects is the release of a DVD edition of his Original Buns of Steel, which will be made available via the Original Buns of Steel official website.
