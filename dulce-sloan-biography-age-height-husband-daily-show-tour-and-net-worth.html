Dulcé Sloan Biography

Dulcé Sloan is an American stand-up comedian, actress, and writer, she was born on July 4, 1983, in Miami, Florida, the United States as Dulcé Lazaria Sloan. Dulcé is a correspondent for The Daily Show with Trevor Noah on Comedy Central.

Dulcé started performing stand-up comedy in May 2009 after being encouraged by friends who worked at the Funny Farm Comedy Club. Dulcé won the 12th Annual StandUp NBC comedy showcase in 2015. Dulcé won the 2016 Big Sky Comedy Festival in Billings, Montana. In February 2016, she performed on Conan O’Brien’s talk show.

Dulcé has appeared on truTV’s Comedy Knockout, @midnight with Chris Hardwick and The Steve Harvey Show. Variety named Dulcé one of 10 Comics to Watch 2018 at Montreal’s Just for Laughs festival. Dulcé is the voice of Honeybee in the Fox animated sitcom The Great North.
