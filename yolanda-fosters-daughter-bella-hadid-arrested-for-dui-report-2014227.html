Yolanda Foster's Daughter Bella Hadid Arrested for DUI: Report

August 21, 2024
post

Not so wise. Bella Hadid, the 17-year-old daughter of Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Yolanda Foster, was arrested early Tuesday, July 22, for driving under the influence and with a suspended license, TMZ reports.

Model Gigi Hadid's younger sister, whose dad is real estate mogul Mohamed Hadid, was reportedly arrested while driving on Pacific Coast Highway around 4 a.m. According to TMZ, she drove through a stop sign and almost collided with an L.A. County Sheriff's Department police car.

 

Law enforcement sources tell TMZ the minor blew a .14, which is almost double the legal limit. The teen was also reportedly driving on a suspended license.
