What Did Matty Healy And Halsey Say About Each Other?

Matty Healy hasn't talked about dating Halsey. He doesn't always mention his romantic life when giving interviews. Sometimes, though, the conversation does turn to who he's currently dating, and he does share a little bit.

Healy mentioned his personal life in a 2018 interview with Vulture. He said, "I’ve always been staring death in the face. Always an existentialist, a nihilist. I just wanna have a baby now. Then I’ll stop doing that."

When the reporter asked about his comment that he would become a parent, he said, "I’m too busy, but do you ever think that sometimes? God! Well, at least then me will go away! I’d like to become a vessel for somebody else’s happiness. That’s where I get meaning in life. I make music; it makes people happy. I find everything else a bit hard. I love my girlfriend."

According to Capitalfm.com, he began dating Gabriella Brooks in 2015 and they broke up in 2019. Since he gave that interview in 2018, it seems that he was referring to Brooks when he talked about "my girlfriend."

In 2015, Halsey talked about Matty Healy in an interview with Billboard. According to Us Weekly, she commented on his attention-grabbing personality and called him a "character." While Halsey didn't go into a lot of detail, it does sound like she and Healy felt that they had some things in common.

Halsey said, "We're both attracted to characters, and we saw that in each other. I spent a lot of time watching him and he spent a lot of time loving being watched. But if you think he’s the first red-wine-­drinking, pretty-boy rocker in skinny jeans I’ve been associated with, you’re out of your f—king mind.”

While he might not want to say anything about Halsey, she doesn't mind praising Healy's talents as a musician. In 2016, she was a guest host on the SiriusFM show HITBOUND and talked about The 1975.

She talked about seeing them perform and said, “There was this choir, and there was these lights, and the sun was going down, and Matty Healy had makeup all over him. It was crazy. But I think the winner of that show was definitely Adam, because he was wearing an incredible suit.”

It also doesn't sound like Halsey has any negative thoughts about Matty Healy. In 2019, she was interviewed on the YouTube Channel AskAnythingChat. According to PopBuzz, she talked about splitting up with Yungblud and said that she is able to maintain platonic relationships with people who she was once romantically involved with.

Halsey said, "Sometimes. People just break up. It doesn’t mean someone cheated or something bad happened or someone f—ed up. Sometimes. It just happens. Because life is constantly changing. And adults stay friends and move on."
