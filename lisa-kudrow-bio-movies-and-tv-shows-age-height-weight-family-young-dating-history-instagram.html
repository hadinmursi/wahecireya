Lisa Valerie Kudrow is an American actress, producer, and screenwriter.

She was born on July 30, 1963 in Encino, Los Angeles, California, United States.

Raised in Tarzana, Lisa graduated from Taft High School in the Woodland Hills neighborhood. She then received a Bachelor of Science degree in Psychobiology at Vassar College in Poughkeepsie, New York.

Lisa initially wanted to pursue a career in research, so she returned to Los Angeles to begin working with her father. However, she got inspired to perform by one of her brother’s friends, comedian Jon Lovitz, and thus entered the show biz.

Lisa Valerie Kudrow rose to prominence for her role as Phoebe Buffay in the American television sitcom Friends, which aired from 1994 to 2004. The series earned her Primetime Emmy, Screen Actors Guild, Satellite, American Comedy and TV Guide awards.

Lisa Kudrow Movies and TV Shows

Lisa Kudrow has starred in several movies and tv shows including In the Heat of Passion II: Unfaithful (1994), Mother (1996), Hacks (1997), Mad About You (1992–1999), Hercules (1998–1999), Wonderland (2003), Father of the Pride (2004–2005), Web Therapy (2008–2014), The Comeback (2005–2014), The Girl on the Train (2016), Feel Good (2020–2021), HouseBroken (2021–2023) and others.
