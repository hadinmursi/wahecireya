Jennifer Hernandez Biography – Jennifer Hernandez Wiki

Jennifer Hernandez was an International Federation of Bodybuilding and Fitness (IFBB) pro women’s physique competitor. According to a biography on her official website, bodybuilder Jennifer Hernandez, says: “I am a role model, fitness competitor, personal trainer, teacher, competitor, mom, grandmother and leader.” She had been a women’s physique competitor since 2005. She was competing as an IFBB pro since getting her pro card in 2009 aged 47. Her last competition was at the Tampa Pro 2021 in August.

Since turning pro, she has competed at the IFBB Tampa Pro 2021, New York Pro 2016, New York Pro 2015, PBW Championships – Tampa Pro 2014, Australian Grand Prix 2012 and the New York Pro 2012.

Jennifer Hernandez Death

Bodybuilder Jennifer Hernandez died in October 2021 at the age of 58. Fellow bodybuilder Dennis James announced her death in an Instagram post. James wrote: “I’m still in shock and can’t believe we lost you Jen. Rest In Peace Jennifer Hernandez. You will be missed forever and always.”

Bodybuilder Milos Sarcev commented under Dennis James’s post, writing: “OMG. I’ve met her through you…She was an absolute sweetheart. Condolences to her family and friends. (When she found out I needed to go to the supermarket, she had her daughter drive me there. An incredible human being. She’ll keep shining on all of us from Heavens. RIP Jen.”
