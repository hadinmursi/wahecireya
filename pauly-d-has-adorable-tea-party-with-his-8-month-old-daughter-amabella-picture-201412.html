Pauly D Adorable &quot;Tea Party&quot; With 8-Month-Old Daughter Amabella: Pic

Pauly D is loving fatherhood. The Jersey Shore alum (real name: Paul DelVecchio) shared an adorable picture of his 8-month-old daughter Amabella playing tea time on Friday, Jan. 31.

"Tea Party With My Princess," the 33-year-old tweeted. Alongside the caption, the DJ shared a sweet picture of his baby girl sitting on the ground next to a play tea set. His cutie pie donned black pants, and rocked a chic pink shirt and fur vest, with a matching bow in her hair.

Us Weekly confirmed last October that the MTV star welcomed Amabella with former Las Vegas one-night fling, Amanda Markert. That same month, Pauly D put in a request for full custody and revealed to Us that he was "nervous" about meeting his baby girl for the first time.

"I was nervous if she wasn't going to like me," Pauly D told Us at the F*ck Cancer NYC Charity Event in NYC on Nov. 21. "But the second I saw the baby her eyes just lit up, it's almost like she knew," he gushed. "It was amazing. I knew she was mine right off the bat too because she looks just like me. She came right over to me and put her arms right up to me. [She] grabbed my hair! That's the first thing she went for. And then she was biting my nose."
