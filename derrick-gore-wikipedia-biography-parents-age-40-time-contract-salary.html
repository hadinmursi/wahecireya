Derrick Gore is an American football running back for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League.

Derrick Gore was born on December 13, 1994, in Syracuse, New York, United States. He went to William Nottingham High School in Syracuse, New York.

Derrick Gore played college football at the University of Louisiana at Monroe (ULM) and the University of Alabama. He began his collegiate career at Coffeyville Community College but transferred to Alabama after receiving an offer to join the team as a walk-on.

Derrick Gore ultimately committed to transfer to Louisiana–Monroe for his final two seasons of NCAA eligibility He was signed by the Los Angeles Chargers as an undrafted free agent on July 24, 2019.

Derrick Gore signed a reserve/futures contract with the Kansas City Chiefs on February 6, 2021. He was signed to the Chiefs active roster on October 12, 2021.
