Bully Ray &amp; Tommy Dreamer vs. Chris Bey &amp; Ace Austin Set For IMPACT Wrestling Bound For Glory Fallou

September 08, 2024
post

Two ECW legends will join forces at Bound For Glory Fallout.

After the Bound For Glory pay-per-view on October 7, IMPACT announced that Bully Ray and Tommy Dreamer would team up to face the Bullet Club duo of Ace Austin and Chris Bey at the Fallout show, which will emanate from the Albany Armory on October 8. Ray returned to IMPACT at Bound For Glory and won the Call Your Shot Gauntlet. In doing so, he earned a title match of his choosing at any time and place.

This tag team bout will be an ECW reunion of sorts, as Ray and Dreamer starred in ECW during the 1990s.
