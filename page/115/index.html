ArgentinaObituaryCarlos GuastavinoThe voice of Carlos Guastavino, who has died aged 88, was, perhaps, the most quietly distinctive in 20th-century Argentinian music. Vigorously rejecting the stylistic radicalism of Alberto Ginastera and his younger compatriot Mauricio Kagel, he followed in the footsteps of 19th-century nationalists such as Julián Aguirre and Alberto Williams. Yet Guastavino was a no less influential figure for the younger generation of composers - particularly in popular music - growing up in Argentina in the 1960s and 70s.
