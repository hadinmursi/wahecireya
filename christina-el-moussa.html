Name:Christina Haack

Hometown:Anaheim, California

Birthday:July 9, 1983

About

Christina Haack gained fame as the host of HGTV’s Flip or Flop with ex-husband Tarek El Moussa in the mid-2000s. The pair worked alongside one another from 2013 to 2022.

Haack was born in Orange County, California and went to college in Southern California before she started a career in real estate, which is how she met El Moussa. The former couple — who ran the real estate company Tarek and Christina: The El Moussa Group until their split in 2016 — share two children: daughter Taylor Reese, born in 2010, and son Brayden James, born in 2015.

Haack and El Moussa filed for divorce in 2016 after eight years of marriage, citing “irreconcilable differences.” Two years later, HGTV announced that Haack would launch her own Flip or Flop spinoff, Christina on the Coast, which documents her life post-divorce as she renovates other people’s homes. The show premiered in 2019.

In 2018, Haack married English TV presenter Ant Anstead at their home in Newport Beach, California, after one year of dating. Their son, Hudson, arrived in 2019. Haack filed for divorce from Anstead one year later.

The designer moved on with Joshua Hall, whom she got engaged to in September 2021. Us confirmed in April 2022 that the duo had quietly tied the knot. The same month, HGTV announced that Haack and Hall would be starring in a new series, Christina in the Country, which set to premiere in late 2022.
