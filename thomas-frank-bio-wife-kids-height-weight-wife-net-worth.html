Thomas Frank is a popular Danish professional football coach and former amateur player, who is head coach of Premier League club Brentford. Frank has been a youth coach for 18 years, which includes being the manager of multiple Danish national youth teams. In 2013, he became a senior manager with Brøndby IF.

Frank became the head coach of Brentford in October 2018. He had been an assistant head coach at the club since 2016. In 2020-21, Frank helped Brentford achieve promotion to the top-flight of English football. This made him only the second head coach or manager in the club’s history to do so.
